◆ How It Works

Our Recycling Process

A streamlined, certified, end-to-end waste management workflow.

📞
Step 01
Request Pickup
Contact us via call, WhatsApp, or our online form. Share details of the waste material — type, quantity, and location. We'll schedule a free pickup at your convenience.
🚛
Step 02
Certified Collection
Our trained team collects waste using proper safety protocols. All items are tagged, documented, and transported in compliant vehicles with full chain-of-custody records.
🔬
Step 03
Sorting & Assessment
Materials are sorted by type — electronics, batteries, metals, plastics. Hazardous components are separated for specialized treatment per CPCB and state guidelines.
⚙️
Step 04
Processing & Recovery
Dismantling, shredding, and material recovery using eco-safe technology. Precious metals, copper, and polymers are extracted and channeled back into manufacturing.
📋
Step 05
Certificate & Report
You receive a full Recycling Certificate with weight, material breakdown, and disposal compliance report — audit-ready documentation for your CSR and ESG reporting.
